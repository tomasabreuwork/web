Me

My name is Tomás Abreu, and I am a 22-year-old member of the Portuguese Air Force. I have always been passionate about technology, which led me to specialize in cybersecurity. My military career has taught me discipline, focus, and the ability to perform under pressure, skills that complement my technical expertise.

I have three years of academic training in programming and two years of hands-on experience in the field. This combination has allowed me to develop strong problem-solving skills and a deep understanding of various coding languages and cybersecurity principles.

My goal is to continue growing as a professional by combining my technical knowledge with the values I’ve gained in the military. I am committed to contributing to the field of cybersecurity and making a meaningful impact in protecting critical systems.
